What is Ad-Apt?

Ad-Apt is the marketing agency I founded and have been running for over 15 years. We work with businesses, brands, and entrepreneurs on the full spectrum of digital marketing — web development, search engine optimization, paid advertising, social media, email marketing, mobile apps, API development, and hosting.

The agency has roots in Socialated, a company I started in 2011 that grew from a social media shop to a 13-person full-service marketing agency before being acquired by Virsocom Ventures in 2014. Ad-Apt is the evolution of everything I learned across that journey — plus a decade of marketing attribution, data-driven strategy, and hundreds of client engagements.

What's Next

Ad-Apt is evolving. The agency model taught me everything about what businesses actually need from marketing — and those lessons are being productized into Onit. The core insight: most businesses need sophisticated marketing but can't afford $10K/month agency retainers. Onit is the answer to that gap — AI-powered execution with the strategic depth of an agency. Ad-Apt continues to serve existing clients while the next chapter takes shape.
