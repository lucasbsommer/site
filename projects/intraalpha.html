What is IntraAlpha?

IntraAlpha is a trading platform built around algorithmic strategies for options and equities. It started as a community-driven product with subscriptions, tools, and resources — and grew into a Discord-based community of traders sharing signals and strategies.

I dedicate time to markets every morning (6:30–8:30 AM) and midday, running the algo and monitoring positions.

Current Direction

  • Simplifying — Disabling most tools and resources, cutting the subscription model
  • Discord-only — Moving to a pure Discord community with algo-only signals
  • Algo migration — Moving the algorithmic strategy to Alpaca via Wealthfront equities
  • Cost reduction — Cutting cloud infrastructure costs significantly
  • Patreon-style model — Exploring a simple support subscription tier

Lessons Learned

IntraAlpha taught me a lot about productizing knowledge, community dynamics, and the difference between building something people want and building something that's sustainable. The algo works. The business model around it needed to be leaner.
