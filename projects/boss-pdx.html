What is Boss PDX?

Boss PDX is a construction company operating in the Portland metro area. We handle residential and commercial projects — everything from concrete and grading to full builds and remodels. The company runs on Zoho for project management, Gusto for payroll, and Ramp for expense tracking.

What makes Boss PDX different is the technology layer. We're building AI-assisted estimating through custom prompts, an online estimate portal for customers, and structured post-mortems on every completed project so future work gets better and more profitable.

How It Runs

  • Project management — Zoho Projects with automated flows for billing, scheduling, and client communication
  • AI-assisted estimating — Custom prompts that generate estimates from project parameters, dramatically reducing turnaround time
  • Customer portal — estimate.bosspdx.com where clients view estimates, project images, and 3D Polycam scans
  • Post-mortem system — Every completed project gets a structured review: scope, budget vs actual, timeline, lessons learned
  • Crew operations — Gusto payroll mapped to projects, Ramp cards per project for expense tracking

Notable Projects

Active and completed projects across Portland: Dilla, Bowden, Timberline, Kirkland, Archer, King, Joe Terrace, WaCo, and more. Each one feeds data back into the system to make the next project smoother.

The Vision

Build the most data-driven construction company in Portland. Every project generates intelligence that makes the next one faster, more accurate, and more profitable. The tech stack isn't overhead — it's a competitive advantage.
