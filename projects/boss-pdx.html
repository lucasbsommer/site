What is Boss PDX?

Boss PDX is a licensed general contractor operating in the Portland metro area with 17+ years of construction experience. We handle residential and commercial projects — from custom home builds and ADU conversions to commercial concrete, excavation, and metal buildings. No job too big or small.

What makes Boss PDX different is the technology layer. The company runs on Zoho for project management, Gusto for payroll, and Ramp for expense tracking — and we're building AI-assisted estimating, an online customer portal, and structured post-mortems on every completed project.

Services

  • Custom home building — Full design-to-build for new residential construction
  • ADU design & construction — Basement, garage, and detached ADU conversions, fully compliant with Portland zoning
  • Commercial concrete — Foundations, slab-on-grade, infrastructure, and large-scale site development
  • Excavating — Land clearing, foundation digging, and trenching
  • Metal buildings — Commercial storage, residential garages, agricultural buildings, workshops, retail spaces, and industrial facilities
  • Pole barn construction — Agricultural, commercial, and residential applications
  • Retaining walls — Residential landscapes and commercial outdoor spaces
  • Commercial remodeling — Tenant improvements, retail buildouts, and office renovations
  • Exterior repairs — Shingles, siding, trim, and garage remodeling

The Tech Stack

  • Project management — Zoho Projects with automated flows for billing, scheduling, and client communication
  • AI-assisted estimating — Custom prompts that generate estimates from project parameters, dramatically reducing turnaround time
  • Customer portal — estimate.bosspdx.com where clients view estimates, project images, and 3D Polycam scans
  • Post-mortem system — Every completed project gets a structured review: scope, budget vs actual, timeline, change orders, subcontractor performance, lessons learned
  • Crew operations — Gusto payroll mapped to projects, Ramp cards per project for real-time expense tracking

Notable Projects

Active and completed projects across Portland: Dilla, Bowden, Timberline, Kirkland, Archer, King, Joe Terrace, WaCo, Camus (Kirkland concrete), Bransford, and more. Each one feeds data back into the system — post-mortems, budget analysis, and operational learnings — to make the next project smoother.

The Vision

Build the most data-driven construction company in Portland. Every project generates intelligence that makes the next one faster, more accurate, and more profitable. The tech stack isn't overhead — it's a competitive advantage. When your estimating is AI-powered and every past project is structured data, you can bid faster, scope tighter, and deliver better than anyone operating on spreadsheets and gut feel.
